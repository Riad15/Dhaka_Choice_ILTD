What we do

B2C

B2C is a kind of product purchasing apps; Customers will be able to purchase products using their apps by connecting directly to the shopper's apps. A customer can avail discounts by purchasing products from any retail store using this app. A shopkeeper can purchase products from any retail store and get discounts and reward points, Such as: grocery store, drug store, clothing store, cosmetic store, food store, restaurant, etc.
